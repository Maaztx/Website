Christian J. Muxica

cmuxica [at] umass [dot] edu

I am a lab manager and research assistant for the UMass Experimental Linguistics Labs. I have done a variety of lab work during my time at UMass, but the bulk of my experience is with eyetracking and acceptability experiments. I am broadly interested in psycholinguistics, but particularly with regard to sentence processing, production, and cognitive modeling. As an undegraduate I completed a capstone involving the use of speeded acceptability judgements to capture different levels of parallelism in comprehension.


Aside from language science I love poetry, making music, and skateboarding.
